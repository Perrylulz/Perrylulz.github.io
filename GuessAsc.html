A Cool Way to Guess Someone's Rising Sign

There's a way to guess someone's ascendant sign that is not foolproof but generally gets you within 3 signs of what it really is. Itâ€™s pretty simple and to do it you need to visualize the natal chart as a clock with the IC being 12 o'clock midnight, the Ascendant being 6 am, MC being 12 noon and descendant being 6 pm

If you have someone whose sun is in cancer and theyre born at 12 o'clock midnight, you can guess that their rising will be aries by counting back signs to the first house or ascendant sign. Keep in mind that if your person is born up north ( like Idaho ) their chart will be skewed and a sign might be eaten up in a house creating for example an earth ascendant and midheaven.

Again, google will come in handy, you might not have guessed the exact sign but it will be that one or the two next to it, by googling you can determine what fits best.
