Remnants

This is a shoot taken in Idaho Falls, ID. The house depicted came to be by a controlled burn by firefighters. Upon being in the house I found old clumps of magazines dated back to the 50's.

I tend to embrace and even prefer the scary or ugly or wierd in my photos, a lot of times these things tend to resonate with me. It's like looking at a really pretty thing haha. The location and the times of these photos are important, every photo I take has some kind of reason or meaning, typically because of the location or for preservation as you can see with this project.
