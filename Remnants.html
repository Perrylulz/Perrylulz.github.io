Remnants

 

These photos are an old house after a fire. After hanging out in there I think the owners died a super long time ago, there were newspapers dated to the 50's.

I love taking photos of emotionally charged things or stuff that induces that, those things tend to resonate with me. It's like looking at a really pretty thing. Sadly the photos are essentially ruined as I was playing around with soome settings and forgot to revert my changes. All of the photos are horribly low quality and in editing I attempted to salavage some of the images but theres nothing I could do. Around this time is where I got into Alice's self titled album. The guitar tones really made an impression on my memory of that time.
