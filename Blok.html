Blok

This is an album featuring the alleyways just behind newbury street. The intended message with this album is to show that seemingly "dangerous" alleyways can be around the corner from the most surveillanced areas. It's also meant to remind us that these alleys are the access to peoples front doors, and are typically safe, especially in Boston.
