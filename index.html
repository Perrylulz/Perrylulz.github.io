R.I.P. Blackstar Amp

Just yesterday my beloved Blackstar Mini Amplifier was blown to bits. I accidentally sat on it :cry:

R.I.P ~ Best crunch I ever had.

On a more serious note, (haha) I had this amp for only a few months. This was the amp that came home with me along with my first electric guitar. Both were bought in July of this year so, obviously, I've been having one hell of a time using it. It's another one of those little objects that I find so valuable in itself, that I made another mock-up coffin as a memorial to it.
